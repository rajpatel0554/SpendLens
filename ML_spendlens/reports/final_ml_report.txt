# 11. Anomaly Detection Module

Function Name:

detect_anomalies(df)

Objective:

Identify unusual spending behavior by comparing current month spending with historical spending trends.

---

# 12. Anomaly Detection Logic

Step 1:
Group transactions by category and month.

Step 2:
Calculate total monthly spending.

Step 3:
Compute average spending of the previous three months.

Step 4:
Calculate spending ratio.

Formula:

Ratio = Current Month Spend ÷ Average Spend of Previous 3 Months

Step 5:
Flag anomaly if:

Current Month Spend > 2 × Previous 3-Month Average

Step 6:
Generate a descriptive reason.

Example:

"Food spend is 3.25x your 3-month average"
