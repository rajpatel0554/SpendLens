SpendLens — Full Project Breakdown
What it does (core features)
The app has 5 main feature modules:
Upload & Parse — user uploads a bank statement (CSV/PDF), the system cleans and structures the data
ML Auto-Categorizer — model classifies each transaction into Food, Transport, Shopping, etc.
Anomaly Detector — flags unusual spends (e.g. 3× your average on dining this week)
Analytics Dashboard — charts, trends, monthly summaries (what you see in the mockup above)
Insight Report Generator — downloadable monthly PDF report with AI-written summary
